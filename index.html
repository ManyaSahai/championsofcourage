About us

About Champions of
Courage

Champions of Courage honors the strength and resilience of individuals facing breast cancer and blood cancer. We share authentic patient journeys—moments of hope, struggle, and triumph—to inspire others and foster a community of support.

Our Goals:

Inspire: Show patients they are not alone.

Educate: Raise awareness of breast & blood cancers.

Empower: Support patients, caregivers & families.

Encourage: Build a growing support network.
